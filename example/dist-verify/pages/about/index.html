About This Example

The purpose of this site is breadth, not depth.

It exists to show that lattice can build:

  • multiple collections
  • paginated indexes
  • tag indexes
  • RSS feeds
  • a sitemap
  • a custom 404 page
  • internal wikilinks
  • schema-validated frontmatter

The content cross-links to release-checkpoint and typed-content-tour so the generated page graph is easy to inspect.

Built to demonstrate structural frontmatter, content collections, and typed site output.
